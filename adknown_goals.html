Goal #1 - Expand my communication skills

  • Goal

    Communication is a life long learning opportunity and is something that as a coop student I can always improve upon. At Adknown there is a lot of emphasis on communicating with the team and with developers in efficient ways, to be able to get effective help fast and an emphasis on asking "good" questions to the right people.

  • Action Plan

    I plan to be profficient in the way I ask questions and to be more accurate in who I choose to direct my questions to within the company. I will be more adament on trying to find an answer to my question before I ask it, and making sure that I am clear, concise and provide the apporpriate examples or previously tested solutions within my question.

  • Measure of Success

    I would think that a good measure of success for this goal would be the success of the project, but a more obscure way of telling that I am meeting this goal is to have my team members communicating openly with myself. If i am doing a good job communicating with my team then I should know when there are issues arising within the project, and not be surprised by them popping up out of the blue.

Goal #2 - Personal Organization and Time Management

  • Goal

    Time management is an extremely important skill for software developers, at Adknown it is an important skill to practise in order to be able to complete tasks efficiently. My goal is to be able to complete tasks more efficiently in order to complete them as close to the estimate time of completion set out by my managers.

  • Action Plan

    In order to complete a task efficiently I must first understand what the task is asking, the scope of work, and an example test case or reproduction steps. I will make an effort to read, understand and analyze the task first, making detailed notes about the aforementioned. If any parts are unclear I will reach out to the appropriate parties for clarification. I will also set up automatic reminders for myself to ensure I am up to date on my time tracking and hours logged each day. Making notes in the time logged about what I worked on / accomplished. Ill be sure to reach out to someone for help if I am stuck on a task for more than 15 minutes, as expected by managers at Adknown.

  • Measure of Success

    I think the best way to measure this goal will be to ask for feedback from my mentor and manager throughout the term.

In terms of becoming a better javascript programmer, there is a lot of practise involved in mastering a language, which I will make sure to seek out as many chances to work with js code as I possibly can during the semester.
