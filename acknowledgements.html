Thank You!

There are many people I would like to thank for the support and advice I have been given over the course of my post-secondary career. First and foremost a big thanks to Laura Gatto, my co-op coordinator who has helped me throughout my career as a co-op student. Laura helped me enter into the co-op program, build a comprehensive resume which helped me land a job as an undergraduate research assistant even before my first co-op work term. Laura has been there to support me during my co-op terms and is always happy to help with any questions or concerns I may have. She is a great mentor and friend!

I would also like to thank Greg Klotz, the coordinator for the school of computer science. Greg has been a tremendous help when it came to choosing courses and deciding my career path, Greg was able to guide me into the proper courses that would benefit me most in what I wished to do after graduation.
