Goal #1 - Develop Knowledge of how websites work

  • Goal

    I would like to become better at communicating with others about systems and processes within the system. I find that when colleagues of mine are speaking in depth about how a system works i can become lost. As well when I try to explain myself to others I can confuse even myself in trying to explain how something I have developed works. 

  • Action Plan

    Each day at Brock we do a scrum where the team gets together and we share what we were working on the previous day, what has us stuck, and any other questions we have that the team ma be able to answer. Scrum gives us all a good chance to catch up with eachother, learn about other parts of the project and ask questions to clarify misunderstandings. I will take full advantage of these scrum sessions in order to improve my oral communication.

  • Measure of Success

    The best way to measure whether or not I have become a better oral communicator is within those scrum sessions, when I explain my process to others and realize that they are following along and understand what I mean, then I will feel like I have achieved this goal. Although it is always something that can be improved
