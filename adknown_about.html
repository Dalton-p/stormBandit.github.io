Adknown is on the cutting edge of web media with a variety of different avenues of work. With an assortment of advertisement technology, traffic distribution management, targeted search portals, web publishing and game development to name a few. The company strives to achieve the highest level of greatness in their developers as possible. Innovative engineering is a very important concept at Adknown, all their technology is developed in house and used on very large scales. Adknown's targeted advertising uses their innovative technology as well as big data to analyse and understand how to engage a specific audience partition. Their in house marketing team is successful because of the broad understanding of different ad implementations to engage with different audiences.

The work environment at Adknown is highly demanding, technical abilities are tested and there is a lot to learn, that being said the company is a great place to work if you are looking for a challenge and to gain a sense of accomplishment. There is a string focus on accomplishing the goals set forth not creating the perfect solutions, this is key to a successful agile environment. Their continuous delivery allows quick changes to be made in production and demands hotfixes to be made on a weekly basis, which keeps the developers on their toes.

The Adknown team understands that all work and no play makes for miserable developers, so to lighten to mood in the office there is a fully stocked bar, a games room and a light comedic atmosphere on the internal messaging system. The various teams also go out for lunch once a week for a social meal where they are able to interact more with the other members and get to know each other, facilitating a friendly culture in the office.
