Goal #1 - Expand my communication skills

  • Goal

    A big part about working in a team to build a new app is communication, the value of communication is high when developing something that has never been done before. I want to be able to improve my written and oral communication skill throughout this work term.

  • Action Plan

    I have implemented a scum methodology with my team, and will be doing a standup meeting with them every single work day. Also I will be using Slack as a communication tool, and Phabricator to develop plans and scrum boards to be used for further communications with the team. I also plan to keep a running log of wiki's on how to complete certain tasks for future onboarding.

  • Measure of Success

    I would think that a good measure of success for this goal would be the success of the project, but a more obscure way of telling that I am meeting this goal is to have my team members communicating openly with myself. If i am doing a good job communicating with my team then I should know when there are issues arising within the project, and not be surprised by them popping up out of the blue.

  • Reflection

    I feel that I have kept up with the daily scrum, and weekly sprint planning meetings well, and done a good job communicating with my team when there is a problem. I also feel that I have been able to make myself available to my team as often as possible when they are in need of assistance.

Goal #2 - Expanding my knowledge of various tools

  • Goal

    Within the project we are using many different tools such as Android Studio, xCode and AWS and languages like Java and Swift. There are a lot of tasks that must be completed in this project that I have no experience with. So my goal is to widen my knowledge and learn a breadth of knowledge about these various tools and languages.

  • Action Plan

    My plan to accomplish this goal is to try and use the tools as much as possible to imerse myself in them and get plenty of practice. I plan to do a lot of reading of the documentation of these tools and playing around with there different implementations and use cases.

  • Measure of Success

    If I am able to make significant contributions to a working product at the end of my term then I will have learned at least a fair amount about these tools and languages.

  • Reflection

    I have spent a lot of time coding in both Android Studio and xCode over the passed few months, and feel that based on my knowledge from before this term and now, I have learned a lot. I feel that my skills with xCode and Swift have become significantly better, as well as the amount of enjoyment I get out of coding for iOS applications has increased significantly.

Goal #3 - Build a ethical and professional team leader attitude

  • Goal

    During this Co-op term I am in the most leadership position I have ever been in for a software project, and that makes me want to be a good leader. I will strive to be a good leader, mentor and ethical teamate for this project.

  • Action Plan

    I plan to be as open as i can with my team and use their strengths to the benefit of the team while being supportive of their weaknesses. I want to see my team grow and evolve in their work and be able to complete tasks without too much stress and with as much help from me as possible.

  • Measure of Success

    One way to measure this goal would be to ask for feedback from my team about how my leadership was during the term and to ask my manager how he thought i did as a mentor as well.

  • Reflection

    I have asked for feedback from my team as well as my manager and formulated a plan for myself to improve even further on these team management skills. The feedback that I recieved was quite positive and I was overwhelmed to hear that my team believed that I was a well suited leader. I am determined to continue my growth as a leader.
